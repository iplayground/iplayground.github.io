歷年現場 Past Editions

2018 → NOW

iPlayground 是從 2018 年開始專為蘋果軟體開發者設計的研討會，由一群資深工程師創辦，目的是彌補台灣在 iOS 開發領域的研討會不足。 iPlayground is a conference designed for Apple software developers since 2018 — founded by senior engineers to fill the gap in iOS-focused conferences in Taiwan.
